# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to an open, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback and resolve disagreements professionally.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, intimidation, or threats.
- Deliberate disruption of community spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct. They may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report concerns to `conduct@codemantle.dev`.
